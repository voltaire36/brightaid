BrightAid

To bridge the gap between aspiring students and compassionate donors, fostering opportunities for growth, education, and success.

About BrightAid

At BrightAid, we believe in the power of community and the potential of education. Our platform is designed to connect international students in Canada with generous donors who want to make a meaningful impact. By addressing financial barriers, we empower students to focus on achieving their dreams and building a brighter future.

"What started as a simple class assignment at Centennial College quickly turned into something far greater. While creating BrightAid for our API class, we realized the profound impact it could have on the lives of international students. BrightAid isn’t just a project—it’s a platform built to empower dreams, connect communities, and make education accessible for those who need it most."

— Voltaire Rono, Co-Founder at BrightAid

Why BrightAid? For Students: A simple, secure way to share your story and raise the funds you need for tuition, living expenses, or emergencies. For Donors: A trusted platform to connect with inspiring individuals and directly contribute to their success.

Our Commitment

Transparency, trust, and empowerment are at the core of everything we do. Whether you're starting a fundraiser or supporting one, BrightAid is here to guide you every step of the way. Join us in transforming lives, one donation at a time. Together, we can make education accessible for all.

As international students, we created BrightAid to help others overcome the challenges we’ve faced.

Kevin

Co-Founder at BrightAid
